Majestic Andaman

With breathtakingly beautiful coastline,lush forested interior,fantastic diving possibilities and a far-flung location,the Andamans islands are a perfect place to ramble around or simply chill out on sunt-toasted beaches.



Shimmering turquoise waters are surrounded by primeval jungle and mangrove forest,and its sugar-white beaches melt under gloriouts flame-and-purple sunsets.

DAY 1
   Arrival at Port Blair

Arrive at the airport and get transferred to your hotel.Later visit the Corbyn's cove beach,and subsequently experience the light and sound show at Cellular Jail.Reture early for an early start on the next day.Overnight at hotel.


DAY 2
   Port Blair to Havelock

Today ,proceed to Havelock in a private ferry.Visit the world renowned Radhanagar Beach before returning to your hotel in Havelock for a comfortable overnight stay.


DAY 3
   Havelock to Port Blair

Today ,return to port Port Blair in a private ferry.Later visit chidiya Tapu ,a pristine evening beach popular for catching the sunset.Overnight at hotel in Port Blair.
